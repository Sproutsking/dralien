❓ FAQ

Everything you need to know about Draliens

Why only 111 supply? ▼
Scarcity creates culture. We believe in building a small, tight alien crew instead of a giant crowd. Utilities grow with community engagement, not supply numbers. This ensures every holder has real value and influence.
What are the royalties used for? ▼
To sustain the community treasury and future development. 1% goes to the Draliens team, 1% to 10 Dralien OG role holders, and 5% for ongoing Draliens development and utility expansion.
What's the difference between WL and FCFS? ▼
There are 100 WL and 100 FCFS slots. Both grant full access to all benefits. WL comes through sales, nominations, giveaways, and reserved slots. FCFS is available by nomination only.
When is the mint date? ▼
Planned for December 30, 2025, depending on Monad mainnet launch. Distribution pace: One WL slot per day until mint day (111 days total), ending just before New Year's Eve.
How do I get the physical 3D Dralien? ▼
We develop 3D models in the Metaverse of your Dralien NFT. These will be 3D-printed professionally, and everyone who mints on mint day will get their unique alien shipped to their home address for free.
How do the monthly airdrops work? ▼
Each holder receives $10 worth of tokens every month for 48 months from small-cap airdrops or ICO allocations. This totals $480+ minimum value, with massive upside potential as these projects grow.
